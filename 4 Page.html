INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY (ICT)

ICT

ICT permeates all aspects of life, providing newer, better, and quicker ways for people to interact, network, seek help, gain access to information, and learn.
Besides its presence everywhere, Information and Communication Technology has an immense economic significance.

ICT EDUCATION

ICT in education improves engagement and knowledge retention:
When ICT is integrated into lessons, students become more engaged in their work.
This is because technology provides different opportunities to make it more fun and enjoyable in terms of teaching
the same things in different ways.
